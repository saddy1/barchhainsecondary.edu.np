E-Library Software

Product Specification  ·  Digital Learning & Library Management System

A web-based platform that brings the library, classroom resources and digital learning into one place. Teachers can build and publish lesson plans, share notes and assign work — while students get a clean, organised space to read, download and submit. Everything is structured by class and subject, with access controlled by role.

Feature Modules

Lesson Plan Studio

Teachers build structured lesson plans with objectives, activities and expected outcomes. Plans can be saved as drafts, published to specific classes, or reused from saved templates.

  • Create & publish by subject and grade
  • Reusable templates
  • Draft & publish workflow
  • Version history
  • Attach resources to plans
Notes & Study Materials

Teachers upload PDFs, slides, images and documents organised by subject, class and topic. Students can read materials in-browser, download files and bookmark items for later.

  • PDF and file upload
  • In-browser reader
  • Organised by class and subject
  • Student bookmarks
  • Visibility control per class
Digital Book Library

A searchable catalog of e-books and reference materials categorised by subject and grade, with access controls ensuring students only see what is relevant to them.

  • Full-text search and filter
  • Category and grade filter
  • Access control by class
  • Student ratings and reviews
  • Trending and recently added
Assignments & Submissions

Teachers create and assign tasks with due dates and point values. Students submit work through the portal, and teachers review, grade and leave written feedback — all within the platform.

  • File-based submissions
  • Due date and alert system
  • Teacher grading interface
  • Written feedback and comments
  • Submission history per student
Announcements & Notices

Publish library and class-level notices targeted by role or class. Supports file attachments, scheduled publishing and read-receipt tracking so you always know who has seen what.

  • Target by role or class
  • File attachments
  • Scheduled publishing
  • Read-receipt tracking
Reports & Usage Analytics

Track which resources students access most, review teacher contributions, monitor submission rates and generate monthly or yearly reports exportable to PDF or CSV.

  • Student activity logs
  • Teacher contribution summary
  • Monthly and yearly trends
  • CSV and PDF export
Smart Search & Discovery

A global search across all content — books, notes, lesson plans and assignments. Students can filter by type, subject, grade or tag and bookmark anything for quick access later.

  • Global search across all content
  • Filter by type, subject and grade
  • Tag-based organisation
  • Personal bookmarks per user

Platform

  • Fully web-based, no installation needed
  • Works on desktop, tablet and mobile
  • Nepali calendar and EN / NE bilingual support
  • Role-based access — each user sees their content
  • Bulk upload for books and resources
  • Email notifications for tasks and notices
  • Integrates with existing School ERP
  • Cloud-ready with secure file storage
